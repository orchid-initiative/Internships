We connect people with purpose.

Orchid Initiative promotes volunteerism, community engagement, and skill development by pairing under-resourced ideas with unlikely collaborators.

Our projects often start as ideas without the team or tooling to grow on their own. Like orchids that need a symbiotic partner to bloom, good ideas need the right connections to take root.

Our role is to bridge that gap — no matter how wide or narrow it may be — through mentorship, creative collaboration, and hands-on projects with volunteers, students, and community members.

Who we are

Orchid was started by Caltech alumni drawn to novel problems — and to mentoring the new approaches it takes to make progress on them. The network spans engineering, healthcare, education, finance and beyond, and stays close to the student experience that shaped it.

One outgrowth of that connection is the Life After Caltech podcast — candid conversations between students and alumni, produced by Orchid.
