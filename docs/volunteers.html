Volunteers

Every role matters.

At the Orchid Initiative, volunteers with various roles work together in a collaborative environment to nurture ideas and drive impactful projects forward.

Approach

From collaborators completing specific tasks to pollinators engaging in conversations or stakeholder roles, each volunteer's unique contribution is essential to our mission of making a difference.

Roles

Three ways to get involved.

Collaborators

Share your time and energy.

What skills are you motivated to practice, develop, or apply?

Work on specific tasks whenever available, providing valuable contributions to ongoing projects.

Pollinators

Share your sense of purpose.

What ideas do you have?

Engage in conversations or act as a stakeholder in defined projects, sharing insights and expertise to drive progress.

Gardeners

Share your experience and influence.

What do you know how to accomplish?

Dive in with consistent involvement, offering mentorship, guidance, and support to projects and team members.
